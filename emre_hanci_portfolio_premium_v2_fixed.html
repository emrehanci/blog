About

I began my journey in the field of software by enrolling in Istanbul University's Computer Programming program in 2013. Successfully completing my education there with a GPA of 3.33, I earned the title of Honor Student. Continuing my education, I opted for Computer Engineering at Hacettepe University in 2016, ranking 610th nationwide in the national exam. In 2019, I started as an intern at Innthebox. Initially working on outsourced projects, I transitioned to a full-time position as my university shifted to remote learning during the pandemic. During this time, I contributed to the Total Energies - ‘Ustaya Özel’ project, utilizing ReactJS, MVC-layered C#, and CRM. In November 2020, I left the company and joined Boni Global. Amidst the pandemic, I took on the role of Full-Stack Developer for the Safe-Steps product, leading the modernization and complete rewrite of the product's management panel using ReactJS. In December 2021, I left the company and joined Authority Partners. Over my approximately two-year journey at Authority Partners, I served as a Full-Stack Developer on two different projects, gaining experience in Angular, AWS, and C#. I accepted an offer from Continental in August 2023. In September 2023, I fulfilled my mandatory one-month military service obligation. Following a lengthy blue card application process, I moved to Hannover in March 2024 and started my new job.
